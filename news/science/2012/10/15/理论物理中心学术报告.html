报告题目：Creation of  hot dense matter:  Quark-Gluon Plasma
报告人：Rudolph C. Hwa 教授，University of Oregon， USA
Abstract:
Heavy-ion collisions at high energy are Little Bangs that create on earth the hot and dense system that existed right after the Big Bang in the beginning of the universe. I shall introduce the subject and describe what can be measured that gives us information about the system called Quark-Gluon Plasma. This is a lecture for a general audience that has no familiarity with high-energy collisions or with quarks and gluons.
