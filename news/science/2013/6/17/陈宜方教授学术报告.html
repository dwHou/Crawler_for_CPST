报告题目： Electron beam lithography and its applications in nanoscience research
报告人：陈宜方教授， 国家千人特聘专家（ 2012 年起），复旦大学信息学院，微电子系； Former Principal Scientist for Nanoscience and Nanotechnology , Micro and Nanotechnology Centre, Rutherford Appleton Laboratory, STFC, UK .
报告摘要
The great success of electron beam lithography (EBL) for deep submicron structures in 1980s initiated the high speed development of nanotechnology for creating mesoscopic systems (ultra small structures from sub microns down to 1 nm) for the study of novel physics, i.e. mesoscopic physic. At the same time other approaches for nanosize features including interference lithography, DUV/EUV lithography and grey scale lithography, etc were also being developed at the highest gear. One decade later, a novel technique which may lead to mass production of nanoprodcuts at economic cost emerged, that is nanoimprint lithography (NIL). This technique was immediately expanded to soft lithography, UV flush NIL, molecular imprint, and flexible template NIL, etc. Even though, the starting point of nanoimprint is still electron beam lithography because the imprint templates have to be fabricated by EBL. Nowadays, nanotechnology has developed beyond lithography regime (pattern transfer), it covers almost every discipline including micro-nano mechanical engineering, bioscience, chemistry and life science, etc. In this talk, I will review the research and development of electron beam lithography and its applications in basic research in nanophysics and nano bioscience. The content will span electron beam lithography basic study, nanofabrications of nanostructures, nanodevices and various nanophotonics. Through this talk, I hope to convince you the new concept of the so-called nanotechnology: it is not only “making things small”, but also, it is to make small things for big functions.
报告人简介
教育经历：陈宜方1982年毕业于复旦大学物理系；1985年获中国科学院上海技术物理研究所固体器件专业硕士学位；1995年获得牛津大学Clarendon Laboratory凝聚态物理博士。
工作经历：陈宜方教授于2012年获得国家千人特聘专家，担任复旦大学微纳科技平台主任（负责信息学院的纳米科技发展），并在微电子系建立自己的科研团队，领导以电子束光刻为核心的纳米科技工作。陈宜方教授在归国前，是英国卢瑟福-阿普尔顿实验室(Rutherford Appleton Laboratory)微纳技术中心(Micro and Nanotechnology Centre) 纳米技术首席科学家(Principal Scientist)，负责该中心的纳米技术与纳米科学的研发，他同时是瑞士洛桑理工大学的客座教授。
科研经历：1985-1990年， 由他领导和首创的三元系半导体平面工艺技术（ 1985-90 ），成功运用于我国风云气象卫星和神舟飞船两项工程，实施这些工程项目的团队获得国家科技进步奖一等和二等奖。 1990 年至今， 分别在牛津大学、荷兰代尔夫特理工大学、新加坡南洋理工大学、英国格拉斯哥大学纳米电子中心和英国卢瑟福实验室微纳中心长期从事纳米科技的前沿工作。他作为主要研究者或者项目负责人（PI），参加或者领导了多项英国工程物理理事会(EPSRC)项目和欧共体第六框架项目。在最近几年， 他在英国主持了多项千万元以上的联合科研项目，包括一项欧共体第七框架科研人员国际交换项目的首席科学家（ 2010-2014 ）、英国新型纳米镜项目协同首席科学家（ Co-Investigator ）（ 2008-2012 ）、英国战略技术发展局纳米技术的防伪安全应用项目首席科学家（ 2007-2010 ）。在国际科技期刊上 发表科研论文130篇。他在电子束光刻、纳米压印和纳米加工技术上所积累的丰富的经验使得他成功地将纳米技术应用于纳米电子学、纳米光子晶体和超构材料以及纳米生物等领域。他是国内863项目的评审专家和长江学者评审专家。
