International Conference on Strings, Particles and Cosmology 2013
弦论、粒子物理和宇宙学2013国际研讨会

*Theme 主题*

The International Conference on Strings, Particles and Cosmology 2013 will be held during 17--21 June 2013 at the Center for Theoretical Physics, Sichuan University. This is the 2nd in a series of annual Strings, Particles and Cosmology meetings. The objective of the conference is to have a gathering of active researchers in string theory, particle physics and cosmology. New progresses in these areas will be discussed and a collaborative platform will be provided. The conference will promote related research activities in China, especially in the Western Region. We welcome local and international researchers and students to attend the conference.
“弦论、粒子物理和宇宙学2013”国际研讨会将于2013年6月17--21日在四川大学理论物理中心举办。这是每年一度举办的弦论、粒子物理和宇宙学会议系列之二。会议旨在聚集一批活跃在弦论、粒子物理和宇宙学领域的学者，研讨相关方面的新进展，并提供一个良好的交流合作平台。藉此会议以推动中国国内特别是西部地区在相关领域的研究。我们欢迎国内外的研究人员和师生光临参加会议。

*Venue 会场*

Lecture Hall room 323, the 3rd floor of the Physics Building, Sichuan University (Wangjiang Campus).  The conference starts at 9:00am on 17 June.
四川大学（望江校区）物理馆三楼323室学术报告厅。会议于6月17日上午9点开幕。
