Title 报告题目:
Dual Cosmos --- Duality between Scale-invariant and Amplitude-invariant Perturbation Spectra in Inflationary and Bouncing Cosmos

Abstract 摘要:

We present a model independent framework to analyze early-universe models.  In view of the latest developments in model building, we promote "m" in the red-blue shift term m*H_\chi into the free parameter.  Combined with \nu, the power-law index of cosmological background, we construct a two dimensional parameter space.  All cosmological models with modified red-blue shift term can henceforth be classified into a point into the parameter space, and the global properties of these cosmological models can be also illustrated:

1) A two-dimensional parameter region of the time-invariance of power spectrum for physical stabilities are obtained;

2) Two group of scale-invariant solutions are delineated inside/outside of the time-invariant region respectively according to their time-dependence;

3) A new duality between scale-invariance and time-invariance of spectra is unveiled, in this parameter space.

As a cosmological application of this new duality, we predict and show that the curvature perturbation of Coupled Scalar-Tachyon Fields Bouncing Cosmos is physically scale-invariant, and it is dual to the Slow-Roll Inflation Model in this new duality.
