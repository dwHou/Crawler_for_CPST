1D Bosons or Fermions in a power trap with repulsive delta function interaction

报告人：马中 骐 教授 中科院高能物理研究所

时间： 2010 年 10 月 27 日 上午 10 ： 20

地点：物理馆 323

Abstract

I will report on the recent works cooperated with Professor Chen Ning Yang. The works are on the coldatom physics.

An N Boson or Fermion system in a 1D power trap |x| a /2 (including the harmonic trap when a = 2 ) with repulsive delta function interparticle interaction is studied. In terms of the Thomas-Fermi method and the results for the trapless system obtained by Lieb and Liniger for Bosons in 1963 and Yang for Fermions in 1967, we calculated numerically the limit of the ground state energy E/N (3a+2)/(a+2) and the limit of the density at fixed x/N 2/(a+2) , as with fixed N a/(a+2) /g for the Boson system, or with fixed N a/(a+2) /g and fixed per particle spin J/N for the Fermion system.

Reference

Ma Zhong-Qi and C. N. Yang

a) Ground state energy for Fermions in a 1D harmonic trap with delta function interaction, Chin. Phys. Lett. 26 (2009) 120505.

b) Spinless Bosons in a 1D harmonic trap with repulsive delta function interparticle interaction I: General theory, Chin. Phys. Lett. 26 (2009) 120506.

c) Spinless Bosons in a 1D harmonic trap with repulsive delta function interparticle interaction II: - Numerical Solutions, Chin. Phys. Lett. 27 (2010) 020506.

d) Spin 1/2 Fermions in 1D Harmonic Trap with Repulsive Delta Function Interparticle Interaction, Chin. Phys. Lett. 27 (2010) 080501.

e) Bosons or Fermions in 1D Power Potential Trap with Repulsive Delta Function Interaction, Chin. Phys. Lett. 27 (2010) 090505.
