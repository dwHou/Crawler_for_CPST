报告人：韩国西江大学 Chai-Ho RIM 教授

报告题目：Irregular conformal block and spectral curve

时间：2015年11月20日星期五下午4点

地点：物理馆323室

报告摘要：I will briefly explain irregular conformal block with Virasoro and W symmetry, which can be obtained from the flow equations of spectral curve (Seiberg Witten curve) corresponding to the symmetry generators.  Irregular random matrix model is employed in the intermediate step to derive the spectral curve and to find the relation between irregular conformal block and regular conformal block.
