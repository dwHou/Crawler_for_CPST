超对称相关物理2016系列讲座

Lecture Series on Supersymmetry Related Physics 2016

【Theme 主题】
The Lecture Series on Supersymmetry Related Physics 2016 will be held from 17 to 29 March 2016 at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.  This meeting is jointly organized by the School of Physical Electronics, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, and the Center for Theoretical Physics, College of Physical Science and Technology, Sichuan University.  Aimed at senior undergraduate students, graduate students and young researchers, a series of pedagogical lectures introducing supersymmetry and its related physics will be provided.  We welcome interested researchers and students to attend the conference.
超对称相关物理2016系列讲座将于2016年3月17日至29日在电子科技大学举办。此次会议由电子科技大学物理电子学院与四川大学物理科学与技术学院-理论物理中心合办，将针对高年级本科生、研究生及青年学者提供一系介绍超对称及其相关物理的讲座课程。欢迎感兴趣的学者师生光临参加。

【Lecture Speaker 讲座报告人】
Tianjun LI 李田军 (Institute of Theoretical Physics, CAS 中国科学院理论物理研究所)

【Venue 会场】
Room 315 of No. 2 Lecture Building, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.
电子科技大学第二教学楼315室。

【Time 时间】
18:30 - 22:00 in the evening, 17, 18, 20, 21, 22, 24, 25, 27, 28, 29 of March
3月17、18、20、21、22、24、25、27、28、29日晚上18:30 - 22:00

【References 参考书目】
"Supersymmertry and Supergravity," by Julius Wess and Jonathan Bagger.
"A Supersymmetry Primer," by Steve Martin.
