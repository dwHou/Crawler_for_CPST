Speaker: Prof. Sung-Soo Kim  金晟洙 教授 (UESTC  电子科技大学)

Title: 5d/6d Superconformal theories and their equivalences

Abstract:
In this talk, I will introduce higher dimensional superconformal theories(SCFTs). Especially I will focus on 5d SCFTs  which can be described from type IIB brane picture, and discuss that the UV completion of two seeming different 5d theories, SU(N+1) and Sp(N) gauge theories with 10 flavors, is in fact equivalent.

Time: 09/14 (Wed) 4:00pm

Venue: Physics building 103, SCU
