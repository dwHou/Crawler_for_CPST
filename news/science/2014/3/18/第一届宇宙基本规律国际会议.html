第一届宇宙基本规律国际会议
The First International Conference on the Fundamental Laws of the Universe

[Theme 主题]

The First International Conference on the Fundamental Laws of the Universe will be held during 21-26 March 2014 at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.  This is a joint conference based on three meeting series in the past:  The "Particle Physics Workshop" held by the Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences; The "LHC Mini Workshop" held by the Center for High Energy Physics, Peking University; And the "Strings, Particles and Cosmology Meeting" held by the Center for Theoretical Physics, College of Physical Science and Technology, Sichuan University.  The objective of the conference is to have a gathering of active researchers in particle physics, cosmology and string theory.  New progresses in these areas will be discussed and a collaborative platform will be provided.  The conference will promote related research activities in China, especially in the Western Region.  We welcome local and international researchers and students to attend the conference.
第一届“宇宙基本规律”国际会议将于2014年3月21 - 26日在电子科技大学举办。此次国际会议是在过去的三个会议系列基础上联合举办，包括由中国科学院理论物理研究所举办的“粒子物理会议”，由北京大学高能物理研究中心举办的“LHC小型研讨会”，和由四川大学物理科学与技术学院-理论物理中心举办的“弦论、粒子物理和宇宙学研讨会”。会议旨在聚集一批活跃在粒子物理、宇宙学和弦论等领域的学者，研讨相关方面的新进展，并提供一个良好的交流合作平台。藉此会议以推动中国国内特别是西部地区在相关领域的研究。我们欢迎国内外的研究人员和师生光临参加会议。
