报告人：加州大学圣芭芭拉分校 许岑珂 教授

报告题目：Interacting Topological Insulator and connections to the Standard Model of Particle Physics

时间：2015年12月14日星期一下午2点

地点：物理馆323室

报告摘要：In the last few years a lot of progresses have been made in understanding the role of interaction in topological insulators (TI) and topological superconductors (TSC).  Now we understand that interaction can reduce the classification of many TI and TSC from $Z$ classification to $Z_8$ and $Z_16$ classification.  In this talk I hope to demonstrate that the interaction-reduced classification of TI and TSC provides us with a possible lattice regularization of the Standard Model of particle physics.  This conclusion is supported by both analytical study and also numerical evidences, with joint efforts from condensed matter and lattice QCD community.
