“千人计划”专家Prof. Aart Kleyn讲座

来源: 本站原创    作者:manager   发布时间:2015/5/4 17:24:01

报告人：Prof. Aart W. Kleyn
中组部“千人计划”外国专家
曾任荷兰物理学会主席
Visiting professor/advisor at Center of Interface Dynamics for Sustainability,Chengdu Development Center for Science and Technology, China Academy of Engineering Physics, Chengdu
摘要 ：
Interactions at surfaces form the basis of many processes relevant for contemporary civilization. Semiconductor manufacturing, heterogeneous catalysis, surface processing and coating, corrosion, and spacecraft reentry are all processes that are happening at and determined by solid surfaces. Therefore it is relevant to obtain knowledge on these processes at the molecular level. There is a wide variety of tools to elucidate the static structure of solid surfaces at the atomic scale. To obtain insight into the dynamics of surface processes is more complicated, because of the femtosecond time scale involved. In this lecture methods available will be briefly introduced and applied to the interaction of nitrogen atoms with Ru(0001) surfaces. The occurrence of direct Eley-Rideal reactions will be shown.In many applications such as nuclear fusion, not only the interaction at the surface is relevant, but also the uptake of gas into the solid. This will be demonstrated for the uptake of D by W, studied by observing the kinetics of the reaction. From this a mechanism for the absorption of D by a W surface will be suggested. Consequences for the fusion reactor wall will be discussed.Finally, a few words will be said about the dissociation of CO 2 in plasma for subsequent surface reactions.
