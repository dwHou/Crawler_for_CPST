共形场论2015系列讲座和研讨会
Lecture Series and Symposium on Conformal Field Theory 2015

【Theme 主题】
The Lecture Series and Symposium on Conformal Field Theory 2015 will be held from 23 November to 3 December 2015 at the Center for Theoretical Physics, College of Physical Science and Technology, Sichuan University. This meeting will have a gathering of active researchers in the area of conformal field theory and string theory. Aimed at senior undergraduate students, graduate students and young researchers, a series of pedagogical lectures introducing conformal field theory will be provided. Topical talks discussing new progresses in these areas will be arranged as well. The meeting will promote related research activities in China, especially in the Southwestern Region. We welcome interested researchers and students to attend the conference.
共形场论2015系列讲座和研讨会将于2015年11月23日至12月3日在四川大学物理科学与技术学院-理论物理中心举办。此次会议将聚集一批活跃在共形场论和弦论领域的学者，针对高年级本科生、研究生及青年学者提供一系介绍共形场论的讲座课程，并安排数次专题报告研讨该领域研究的新进展，藉此以推动发展中国国内特别是西南地区在相关领域的研究。欢迎感兴趣的学者师生光临参加。

【Venue 会场】
Lecture Hall 323 of Physics Building, Sichuan University.
四川大学物理馆323学术报告厅。
