Seminar Talk at CTP-SCU / 理论物理中心学术报告

Speaker: Prof. Chong-Sheng Li （李重生教授） (Peking U.  北京大学)

Title: Cross sections with a Jet veto

Abstract:

In this talk, I will report the recent progress on PQCD at the LHC, especially on resummation with jet veto in the framework of soft-collinear effective theory, which mainly involves the processes of Higgs and vector boson associated production, gauge boson pair production and top quark pair production.

Time: 6/26 (Fri) 3:00pm

Venue: Physics building 103, CTP-SCU
