Dr. Lei Zhu 学术报告
报告时间：2015年6月23日上午10:00
报告地点：原子核科学技术研究所 学术报告厅(二楼)
报告题目：MIRTL research highlights
主讲人  ：Dr. Lei Zhu
George W. Woodruff School of Mechanical Engineering
Georgia Institute of Technology
主讲内容： This talk introduces various research topics developed in the Medical Imaging and Radiation Therapy Laboratory (MIRTL), a research facility directed by Dr. Lei Zhu at Georgia Institute of Technology. Dr. Zhu’s current research is focused on advanced imaging techniques for diagnosis, prognosis, treatment guidance, real-time tumor tracking in radiation therapy, and nano-scale biological studies. This presentation covers several recent techniques to address challenges in the above fields. Some early patient studies have demonstrated the clinical values of the proposed methods.
