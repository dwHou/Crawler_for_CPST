Seminar Talk at CTP-SCU / 理论物理中心学术报告

Speaker: Prof. Runqiu Liu （刘润球 研究员） (AMSS, CAS 中科院数学院)

Title: A pedestrain introduction on gravitational wave detection and numerical relativity

Abstract:
I will first give a brief description of the physics underlying gravitational wave, its detection and the possible repercussions in high energy astrophysics. An overview is then given on the subject of numerical relativity and my personal view of its future development.

Time: 6/4 (Thu) 4:00pm

Venue: Physics building 323, CTP-SCU
