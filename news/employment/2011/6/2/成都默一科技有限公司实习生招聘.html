成都默一科技有限公司实习生招聘

来源: 本站原创    作者:学工组   发布时间:2011/6/2 18:25:16
成都默一科技有限公司是一家设立于高新孵化园的集成电路设计公司。 公司是一家创新型的设计公司，专注于高性能智能消费类集成电路的研发。公司定位于给消费类 市场提供以高速ADC为核心的智能控制IC，诸如LED照明控制、太阳能IC、触摸屏控制、交互式智能音频放大器等领域。
本次招聘对象：微电子类硕士研究生。
Monolithic Silicon limited is an innovative fabless design house that focuses on high-performance intelligent consumer integrated circuits.
With its inception in 2011, the company promises to design robust and efficient IC products into consumer market, such as LED lighting controller, Solaris IC product, touch screen controller, intelligent interactive audio amplifier... The startup team is a diverse group of recognized leaders who are focused on IC design with robust quality and support to a worldwide customer base.
实习工作职位要求： E xperience using Cadence simulation tool needed; and experience in CMOS process and design of op-amp, bandgap, voltage reference and comparator preferred. Responsibilities include analog circuit design, low & high voltage CMOS circuit design, SPICE simulation,etc.
有意向者可与成都默一科技有限公司马超经理联系， ms@mono-si.com
