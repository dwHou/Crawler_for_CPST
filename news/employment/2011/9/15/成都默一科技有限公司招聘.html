成都默一科技有限公司是一家创新型的集成电路设计公司，设立于成都高新孵化园。公司专注于高性能智能消费类集成电路的研发，在LED照明控制、太阳能IC、传感器智能控制、交互式智能放大器等领域，给消费类电子市场提供以高速ADC为核心的智能控制IC。
Monolithic Silicon limited is an innovative fabless design house that focuses on high-performance intelligent consumer integrated circuits. With its inception in 2011, the company promises to design robust and efficient IC products into consumer market, such as LED lighting controller, Solaris IC products, intelligent sensor controller, intelligent interactive amplifier... The startup team is a diverse group of recognized leaders who are focused on IC design with robust quality and support to a worldwide customer base.
