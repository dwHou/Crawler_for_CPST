1. 科研位置
Positions are available for graduate students, postdoctors, research scientists, engineers, and faculty members at the Peac Institute of Multiscale Sciences (PIMS). PIMS is a new research and education institute with focus on understanding materials properties at multiple temporal and spatial scales, in particular at submicron and subnanosecond scales. The subject areas span over materials science, mechanics, condensed matter and plasma physics, chemistry, optics including THz, X-ray sciences, and biomedical sciences. All candidates with expertise in experiment, numerical simulation, modeling or theory will be considered, and are expected to have genuine interest in scientific research, intellectual curiosity and integrity, as well as the spirit of teamwork.
PIMS is located on the beautiful Jiang'an campus of Sichuan University, in Chengdu, China. PIMS adopts rigorous, western-style systems for graduate education and research. PIMS will supply internationally competitive research essentials (infrastructure, funding, and administrative support), salaries and benefits. Outstanding candidates will be considered for “1000-Talent Plan" (senior and junior), or other prestigious positions. If interested, please send your CV, and the names of three references, to Dr. Sheng Luo, at sluo@pims.ac.cn .
2. 文秘位置
An administrative position (junior or senior secretary) is to be filled immediately at the Peac Institute of Multiscale Sciences (PIMS). All candidates will be considered regardless of gender, age and background, and are expected to have extraordinary communication skills (spoken and written), professionalism, self-motivation, etiquette, and a good command of English.
PIMS is located on the beautiful Jiang'an campus of Sichuan University, in Chengdu. PIMS will supply competitive working environment, salary and benfiets. If interested, please contact Mr. Li at junpeng_li@pims.ac.cn with CV and references, or phone (028)85993168.
