Position Description:

§          Be capable of data analysis, gathering, processing and other analytical tasks under direction

§          Be able to communicate and present findings and ideas in a clear, focused and persuasive manner

§          Have ability to achieve results in a team environment

Some skills are necessary for you to develop in consulting industry:
