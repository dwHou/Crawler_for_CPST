Prof. Zheng Yuanjin from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. There are following positions available in his group:

a.  Ph.D. scholarship

There are Ph.D. scholarships available on the topics of: 1. Biomedical sensing and imaging; 2. IC design for Bio-sensor for 2015 Aug. intake.

b.  Postdoctoral/research fellow/ research engineer

There are several postdoctoral/research fellow (Ph.D degree holder) and research engineer (B.E./M.E. degree holder) positions available. Those candidates with following background (one or more) are welcome to apply:

• Biomedical sensing and imaging physics and systems
• RF and analog IC design for radar/communication

If interested, please send your email to YJZHENG@ntu.edu.sg for enquiry and application at earliest time. See http://www3.ntu.edu.sg/home/yjzheng/ for more details.
